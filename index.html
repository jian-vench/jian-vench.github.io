About our Cafe Monster

Hi Customers!

My name is Jian Vench V. Palculan, the owner of Cafe Monster.

As a student, my friends and I really really wanted to find or hunt for a place, a cafe specifically, that had the affordable goods and a student-free environment where we could study and hang out at the same time. Unfortunately, we haven't been able to find a cafe with the qualities we're looking for, which is the main reason I started this business as a place for students to study and hang out with their friends at the same time. Additionally, the products we offer are specifically designed for students with limited funding, Furthermore, we want to assist underprivileged students by hiring them to work with us full- or part-time while providing excellent benefits for them.
